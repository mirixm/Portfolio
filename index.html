MY PROJECTS

I found my passion for stop motion at the beginning of 2018. Back then, I created myself with a simple app and started making videos. Back then, I only made videos of my best friends or of my experiences. I started with YouTube in mid-2018 and uploaded the videos there, and less than 2 weeks later, I had already cracked my 100 subscribers on YouTube. I uploaded more and more, and after 3 years, I had 8457 subscribers on YouTube and also earned my first pocket money. But because of all the video production, I was no longer keeping an eye on my school grades, so I had to stop and concentrate on school.

During the Corona pandemic, I was more interested in animation and wanted my character to move better and cleaner. I taught myself how to animate characters. I was able to work better for school in the home office and also had good grades, so I started uploading videos again, but unfortunately, my subscribers had dropped, and I only had 5600 subscribers during this period

In 2022, I stopped using YouTube completely and therefore lost my monetization, but I didn't care. I wanted to create my own story, and I did. I made my story privately on another phone, and I used any free time every night or in the afternoon to continue working on my story. I told my best friends about my story, and they all loved it and asked me why I didn't publish it. I then said that somehow I didn't want to because I wasn't finished yet and didn't really want that other people see my story.

Now it's 2025, and I'm still working on my story. I uploaded 1-4 min videos at the beginning of 2024. Well, as my friends were right, my story is exciting.
